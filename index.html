RolloutPTSD Connect Guide

Registering:

After registering, the list will at minimum be two channels: the global "RolloutPTSD" channel that everyone is a member of and at least one location channel. If you identified yourself as a particular service type, you will have those channels also.

If you change your profile service type, it will change your membership to those channels (leave the current if they're different) and add you as a member to new service channel types.

Changing your location(s) will also change your channels and memberships from current to new channels for those locations.
