Introduction:

Welcome to RolloutPTSD Connect! We appreciate your trust and are committed to safeguarding the confidentiality and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy elucidates how we gather, employ, and secure your data when you utilize our mobile application.

Information We Collect:

  • Personal Information:
    • Examples: Name, Email Address (not shared)
  • Device Information:
    • Examples: Device Model, Operating System (not shared)
  • Usage Data:
    • Examples: User type (only shared with event admins), Event update type (only shared with with event admins)
  • Service type Data:
    • Examples: Military service type data, First responder type data
  • General location and current location Data:
    • Examples: Service location data, Non-specific residence location data, Current location data (not shared)

We employ various methods to collect data, including:

  • Direct User Input: When you provide information during account creation or in-app interactions.
  • Automated Means: Through geocoding and native device hardware.
  • Third-Party Integrations: Google Maps API, Facebook (shared link to our FB page)
  • Purpose of Data Collection: We collect and utilize data for the following purposes:
      • a. Providing and maintaining the app’s functionality.
      • b. Personalizing your experience.
      • c. Sending notifications and messages.

      Data Usage and Sharing

      We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with third parties to assist with app-related services. You have the right to:

      • Access your profile data.
      • Correct profile inaccuracies.
      • Replace your profile data.
      • Manage your notification setting within the app and on your device.

    Security Measures

    We implement robust security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your data from unauthorized access and misuse.

    • Updates and Changes: This Privacy Policy may be updated, and you will be notified of any significant changes. Your continued use of the app after such modifications implies your consent.
